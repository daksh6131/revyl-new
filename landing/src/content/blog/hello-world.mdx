## Why AI-Powered Testing?

Traditional mobile testing is slow and brittle. Element locators break constantly. Writing and maintaining test scripts takes more time than the actual testing. And scaling across devices? Forget about it.

We're changing that with Revyl. Our AI understands your app like a human would—it can:

- **Find elements by description**, not fragile XPath selectors
- **Adapt to UI changes** without breaking your tests
- **Execute natural language instructions** so anyone can write tests
